# ADR-0001 Product Wedge

## Status

Accepted

## Decision

Do not build a pure biological-age product first.

Build a preventive / longevity evidence layer that treats biological age as a marketable wedge inside a broader workflow:

- ingest source reports
- normalize canonical concepts
- preserve provenance
- support modality-aware review
- track interventions over time

## Consequences

- the product remains useful even if aging scores commoditize
- the core moat shifts toward longitudinal evidence and workflow fit
- feature work should favor normalization and review over flashy aggregate scoring
